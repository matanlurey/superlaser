CardOrVariant

CardOrVariant class sealed

Represents either a CanonicalCard card or a VariantCard of a card.

Comparison

CardOrVariants are compared by their Card.numbers, in ascending order.

Equality

Two CardOrVariants are considered equal if their card is equal.
