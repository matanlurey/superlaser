Card

Card class sealed

Any card in Star Wars: Unlimited, with a sealed hierarchy of card types.

The hierarchy is sealed as the following:

Card
├── LeaderCard
├── BaseCard
├── TokenCard
└── PlayableCard
      ├── UnitCard
      ├── UpgradeCard
      └── EventCard

In addition, ArenaCard, DeckCard, TargetCard, PowerCard, AttachmentCard are sealed interfaces that are implemented by some of the above classes in order to indicate shared behavior.

Equality

Two cards are considered equal if they:

  • Both are TokenCards or both aren't TokenCards;
  • Have the same expansion and number.
