PlayArea

PlayArea class final

Consists of all^1 cards that a player controls in Zone.inPlay zones.

A player's play area includes cards in that player's baseZone, cards in that player's resourceZone, units that the player controls in the groundUnits or spaceUnits, and any upgrades attached to units that the player controls.

^1: With the exception of any upgrades that a player controls attached to enemy units (which are in the unit's controller's play area).
